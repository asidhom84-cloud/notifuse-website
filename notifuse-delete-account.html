Last updated: June 23, 2026

Notifuse Account Deletion Request

Users may request deletion of their Notifuse account and associated personal data by contacting:

Email: support.notifuse@gmail.com

Email subject: Delete My Notifuse Account

Please include: the email address associated with your Notifuse account.

What will be deleted

  • Notifuse account
  • Saved alerts
  • Notification preferences
  • Associated user profile data

Data that may be retained

Records required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or dispute resolution, if applicable.

Processing time

Deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days.

Privacy Policy

For more information about how we handle personal data, see our Notifuse Privacy Policy.
